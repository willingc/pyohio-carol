## Direction of talk

Today's world is unpredictable and feels like it is changing at an accelerated rate. All of my life the world has been changing at an accelerated rate. The difference is that we are able to move information around the world virtually instantaneously and at a scale greater than before.

Building a Python developer takes resilience, effort, and community. Today I want to share with you some tips that I learned while building in Python and its community.

Three golden rules. Three slithering rules.
- Adapt to change
- Open doors
- Be humble
